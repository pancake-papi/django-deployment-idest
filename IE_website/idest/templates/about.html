IE Art Historians

Id Est is a online zine dedicated to make a history of the artists and musicians from the Inland Empire.
Our goal is to make IE art more accessible for audiences to find through this zine, as well as give an outlet for IE artists to expose their work.
We hope that doing this leads to artists finding a wider audience which further allows the Inland Empire artists to flourish.
